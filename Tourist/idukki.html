Welcome to Idukki

Idukki, nestled in the Western Ghats of Kerala, is a haven of natural beauty and biodiversity. It's known for its majestic hills, sprawling tea gardens, dense forests, and breathtaking dams. The Idukki Arch Dam, one of the highest in Asia, is a major attraction. The district offers a tranquil escape for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.

Tourist Destinations in Idukki

Idukki Arch Dam

Asia’s first and the world’s second arch dam, built across the Kuravan and Kurathi hills, is a marvel of engineering and a scenic wonder.

Munnar

Famous for its tea plantations and cool climate, Munnar is a hill station in Idukki offering lush green landscapes and mist-covered valleys.

Thekkady

Home to the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, Thekkady offers boat safaris, bamboo rafting, and sightings of elephants and other wildlife.
