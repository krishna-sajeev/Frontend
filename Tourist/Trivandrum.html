Welcome to Thiruvananthapuram

Thiruvananthapuram, the capital of Kerala, is a city where heritage meets modernity. Home to ancient temples, scenic beaches, and vibrant art culture, it offers a perfect blend of tradition and tranquility.

Tourist Destinations in Thiruvananthapuram

Padmanabhaswamy Temple

One of the richest and most sacred temples in the world.

Kovalam Beach

Iconic lighthouse, clean beach, and surfing spots.

Veli Tourist Village

Ideal for family outings with lake, pedal boats, sculptures.

Napier Museum

Indo-Saracenic architecture and rich collection of art and history.

Poovar Island

Backwaters meeting the sea, boat rides, floating cottages.
