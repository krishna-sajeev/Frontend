Welcome to Wayanad

Wayanad, nestled in the Western Ghats, is a serene hill district in Kerala known for its lush greenery, misty mountains, waterfalls, and rich tribal culture. It’s a perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Tourist Destinations in Idukki

Banasura Sagar Dam

India’s largest earthen dam, scenic boating spot

Edakkal Caves

Prehistoric rock engravings and panoramic hill views.

Soochipara Waterfalls

Three-tiered waterfall ideal for trekking and swimming.

Chembra Peak

Famous for the heart-shaped lake and trekking.

Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary

Rich in flora and fauna, part of the Nilgiri Biosphere.
